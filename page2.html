C.B. Potts

We are all about great food, ice-cold hand-crafted beer and creating memories, and we have been for nearly 40 years. In 1974, the first C.B. & Potts opened as a “Deluxe Tavern” in Fort Collins. Today, we operate seven locations throughout Colorado – as well as our sister brand, The RAM, in the Midwest and Pacific Northwest.

Moot House

Located in the heart of Fort Collins, The Moot House is all about fresh, local flavors and good times. That’s because we’re passionate about the food we serve. From our beer-inspired menu that features wholesome, sustainable ingredients to mouth-watering meals that are made from scratch – right when you order, we tap into that fresh from the farm taste and pair it with a chef-inspired, artisan flair. So grab your friends, and get ready to experience the best taste of the town. With over 30 beers on tap, we’re sure this is one English pub you’ll want to visit time and again.

Rally King

Reinvention and fulfilling your dreams… that’s what Rally King is all about. Former home brewer Matt Kriewall caught the brewing bug in 2009. Hundreds of batches of beer and many home brewing awards later, Matt turned his hobby and passion into a profession. Rally King Brewing opened its doors in 2015 and has become a favorite neighborhood brewery for Midtown locals and other craft beer lovers. Located right off the Spring Creek trail with a pet-friendly patio, and 18 beers on tap (including three sours), Rally King has something for everyone!
